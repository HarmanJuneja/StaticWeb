Goan Fish Curry

A spicy, tangy, and flavorful coastal delight

Ingredients

  • 500g firm white fish (pomfret, kingfish, or mackerel)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 2 tsp coriander seeds
  • 4 dried red chilies
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp tamarind pulp
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Grind coconut, coriander seeds, cumin, red chilies, and turmeric into a smooth paste using a little water.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions until golden brown.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Add the ground masala paste and fry for a few minutes.
  5. Pour in 1½ cups of water and bring to a gentle boil.
  6. Add fish pieces and salt; cook on low heat for about 10 minutes.
  7. Stir in tamarind pulp and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Tips

  • Use fresh coconut for the best flavor.
  • Adjust chilies according to your spice preference.
  • Kingfish or pomfret works beautifully with this curry.
